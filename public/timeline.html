May 18, 2026

Another Sweep Across the Edges

A second pass of small, mostly-invisible hardening: kept dependencies fresh, gave the operational plumbing a bit more discipline, and resolved a handful of subtle behaviors that were too generous. May 18, 2026

A New Coat of Paint — and Then Some

UmmahCast got a deliberate visual refresh. Headings now use Amiri, a classical Arabic-inflected serif that pulls the look toward mosque architecture rather than generic tech-startup. The palette shifted from cold slate to warm sandstone (light) and deep, earthen tones (dark). A subtle muqarnas-inspired pattern — echoing the layered vaulting found in mosque ceilings — runs quietly through hero sections and dividers. Corners are sharper and more intentional on big blocks. The goal: look like an Islamic platform that happens to be modern, not a modern platform with Islamic stickers.
